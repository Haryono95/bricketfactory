ABOUT US

Global Leader in Charcoal Export

CharcoBrik is a trusted exporter of high-quality coconut shell charcoal briquettes based in Indonesia. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern production techniques to deliver consistent, premium-grade products for international buyers.

Our briquettes are known for their long burning time (2-3 hours), zero smoke, no smell, and ash content below 2%. We cater to shisha lounges, restaurants, and distributors worldwide.
